Our Story

Smart Waste. Stronger Communities.

WastElimna was founded on a simple but powerful belief: waste management can do more than remove rubbish. It can protect ecosystems, create jobs, empower communities, and drive lasting change.

From residential collections to large-scale commercial operations, we bring innovation, reliability, and a genuine passion for sustainability to everything we do.

Our GP-PACE initiative extends our impact further — advancing peace, strengthening communities, and promoting circular economy solutions around the world.
