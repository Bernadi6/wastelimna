What is GP-PACE

Beyond Waste Management

GP-PACE is WastElimna's non-profit partner initiative. While our core business handles waste, GP-PACE addresses the deeper social and economic conditions that perpetuate environmental harm.

Working in some of the most challenging environments worldwide, GP-PACE brings together local communities, NGOs, government bodies, and businesses to create lasting change through circular economy principles.

The initiative is built on three pillars: peace-building, economic empowerment, and sustainable resource management — because a clean environment and a peaceful society go hand in hand.

Our pillars

Three Pillars of Change

01

Peace Building

Creating dialogue and cooperation between communities through shared environmental projects. When communities work together around clean environments, conflict decreases.

02

Economic Empowerment

Training local entrepreneurs in waste collection, recycling, and upcycling businesses. Turning environmental challenges into economic opportunities for local people.

03

Circular Economy

Building systems that keep resources in use for as long as possible. From composting food waste to recovering metals, every material has a second life.
