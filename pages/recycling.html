How we recycle

Smarter Sorting, Better Recovery

We collect materials separately to maximise recovery rates. Our state-of-the-art sorting facilities process paper, glass, plastics, metals, electronics, and organics — ensuring as much as possible stays out of landfill.

We partner with certified processors and track every tonne to give you transparent recycling reports you can use in your sustainability disclosures.

Recycling Tips

✓

Rinse containers before recycling — food residue contaminates whole batches.

✓

Flatten cardboard boxes to save space in your recycling bin.

✓

Keep lids on plastic bottles — most lid types are now recyclable together.

✓

Never put black bin bags inside your recycling — they block sorting machines.

✓

Batteries and electronics need separate collection — contact us to arrange.
